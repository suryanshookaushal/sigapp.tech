    About us


    With the motto of promoting technology, creativity and innovation, We at SIG-APP understand and believe that this fast changing and evolving era is the perfect time to live in and step-up to shape our future by looking at the world as our playground!

    We are SRMIST's official ACM Special Interest Group for Applied Computing. Our focus is on research and development of various applications of Artificial Intelligence and Big Data towards real world problems. Not only do we indulge in great in-house projects but also work alongside experienced proffessors and company offcials to develop state of the art solutions. We also house skilled Web and App developers who assist the group in turning research intro production.
